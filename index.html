03 — Case Files
Case File — Identity & Property
Smart Government Property Transfer Platform
Flutter · Laravel · FastAPI · TimeSformer · MTCNN · OCR · KYC
Team project — my role: Frontend & ML Engineer
  • Collaborated with a team to design and build a full end-to-end digital government platform enabling citizens to transfer residential real-estate ownership entirely online, integrating four government entities — judicial registry, land registry, finance authority, and a mock bank — into a single digital workflow.
  • Built the Flutter front end that carries a citizen through the transfer flow, from document submission through the KYC challenge to final ownership-claim issuance.
  • Trained a TimeSformer-Base video transformer on Celeb-DF v2 (6,240 videos, identity-split 75/10/15%) to detect face-swap deepfakes as part of the platform's liveness check.
  • Engineered a GPU-accelerated face-extraction pipeline with MTCNN producing 224×224 aligned crops across 8 frames per video, trained with AdamW and gradient clipping, BCE loss with class-weight balancing, and early stopping (patience 5).
  • Helped integrate the multi-stage KYC pipeline — OCR-based national-ID extraction, a face-liveness challenge via the deepfake classifier, and OTP confirmation — to block identity spoofing before any sensitive transaction proceeds.
  • Contributed to a 15-stage transaction state machine (create → judicial check → buyer approval → valuation → tax calculation → payment → OTP → completion → ownership-claim code) with strict sequential gating.
